\n# Plan: Verification Fast-Track + Marketing Studio Credits\n\nTwo new revenue streams. Both reuse existing Stripe infrastructure (one-time checkout pattern from Featured Placements, subscription/topup pattern from Pro plan). iOS native UI hides all payment surfaces per platform constraint.\n\n---\n\n## Part 1: Verification Fast-Track Fee ($49 / $99)\n\n**Goal:** Businesses pay to skip the standard 24–48h verification queue. Pure-margin one-time fee, no ongoing cost.\n\n### Tiers\n- **Standard** — Free, 24–48h SLA (current behavior, unchanged)\n- **Priority $49** — Reviewed within 4 business hours\n- **Same-Day $99** — Reviewed within 1 business hour, M–F 9–6 CT\n\n### Database\nNew columns on `business_verifications`:\n- `priority_tier` text — `'standard' | 'priority' | 'same_day'`, default `'standard'`\n- `priority_paid_at` timestamptz\n- `priority_stripe_session_id` text\n- `priority_sla_deadline` timestamptz — computed on payment\n\nNew table `verification_priority_payments` (audit trail, admin RLS).\n\n### Edge functions\n- **`create-verification-priority-checkout`** — one-time Stripe Checkout (`mode: payment`), accepts `tier` (`priority`/`same_day`) and `business_id`. Validates the caller owns the business and a verification exists in `pending` status. Returns checkout URL.\n- **Extend `stripe-webhook`** — on `checkout.session.completed` with metadata `type=verification_priority`, write `priority_tier`, `priority_paid_at`, compute `priority_sla_deadline` (now + 4h or +1h business hours), insert audit row.\n\n### Frontend\n- New component `VerificationPriorityUpsell.tsx` shown in `VerificationTab` and inside `BusinessVerificationStatus` (only when status is `pending` and not already paid).
