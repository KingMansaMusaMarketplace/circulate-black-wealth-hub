## The Onboarding Strategy (plain English)

AAMES is a volunteer-run org with **old paperwork-based systems** and **7 named stakeholders** each owning a different workstream (rosters, payments, comms, youth tools, security, gamification, digital equity). If we drop a generic SaaS login in front of them, they will not adopt it — volunteers don't have time to learn software.

So we run a **white-glove, phased rollout** built around three principles:

1. **We do the work, they approve it.** For the first 30 days, 1325.AI staff + Kayla agents migrate their existing paperwork into the system *for them*. Volunteers only have to review and sign off.
2. **One stakeholder, one workstream, one win per week.** Instead of training all 7 leaders at once, we onboard them one at a time with a visible win (e.g., Week 1 = Tony sees every Scout roster digitized and searchable).
3. **Member-network monetization runs in parallel.** While HQ onboarding happens, we spin up a co-branded **AAMES → 1325.AI Pro signup funnel** ($299/mo) that Dr. Hill can push to the 2.5M member list and affiliated business owners. AAMES gets a revenue share or sponsor credit — your call which.
