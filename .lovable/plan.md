

## Plan: Reframe copy to avoid "replacing human labor" language

**What changes:**

1. **Hero description** (line ~456-457 in `BusinessSignupPage.tsx`):
   - Change from: *"28 autonomous services that replace $12,100+/month in human labor. She manages your marketing, finances, operations, and community impact — so you can focus on what you do best."*
   - Change to: *"28 autonomous services that save you $12,100+/month in time and overhead. She handles your marketing, finances, operations, and community impact — so you can focus on what you do best."*

2. **SEO meta description** (line ~418):
   - Change from: *"...replacing $12,100+/mo in human labor..."*
   - Change to: *"...saving you $12,100+/mo in time and overhead..."*

3. **"~4 FTEs Replaced" stat** → Change to **"~4 Roles Covered"** (softer, same meaning without the "replacement" framing)

**Why Option B works best:**
- "Time and overhead" is neutral — no implication of job loss
- "Handles" instead of "manages" feels more like a tool assisting you
- "Roles Covered" shows capacity without triggering fears about displacement
- Keeps the $12,100+ figure front and center for ROI impact
